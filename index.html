Other Hobbies

Linguistic

In my view, linguistics is the most fascinating subject within the humanities as it encompasses so many diverse aspects. It includes history, culture, interesting puzzles and intellectual problems, and at times it can be quite philosophical.
As an Iranian, I am particularly interested in the evolution of Persian, the most widely spoken Iranian language. More specifically, I consider myself a Persian Purist which is a movement dedicated to revitalizing the Persian language. We seek to preserve the linguistic integrity of Persian by reintroducing ancient and often forgotten Middle Persian words and by constructing new terms as substitutes for foreign loanwords. Personally, my ultimate goal is to use more original Persian words within scientific and technological contexts.
